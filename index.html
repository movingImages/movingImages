Art from the past

We, as a group, were incredibly fascinated by the complexity and elegance of art in the 18th and 19th centuries. Neoclassicism and Rococo especially stood out. Unfortunately, too few people, especially the young society are not interested in the art... The main factor why the paintings are considered to be boring is that it is necessary to observe and understand the paintings. Most people find it difficult, so we give the paintings a new update, so that the paintings are even more interesting and easier to understand.
